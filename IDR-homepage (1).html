Who We Are

Built by Industry,
Backed by Academia

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles within regulated and high-consequence environments — including financial services, critical national infrastructure and healthcare.

"We close the gap between academic knowledge and the operational demands of modern digital risk."

Through exclusive UK university partnerships, IDR delivers programmes that combine cutting-edge academic insight with hands-on industry projects, ensuring every practitioner we train and place is genuinely work-ready from day one.

Our Community

Who We Serve

IDR exists to grow and sustain a professional community of digital risk practitioners — from early-career entrants to experienced professionals seeking to upskill in specialised, high-demand domains.

Our community spans sectors where digital and cyber risk is not an afterthought — it is mission-critical.

  • Students and graduates seeking a clear, industry-validated pathway into digital risk roles
  • Early-career professionals transitioning from adjacent disciplines such as IT, compliance or audit
  • Experienced practitioners in financial services, critical infrastructure and regulated industries seeking advanced upskilling in AI, cloud and supply-chain risk
  • Organisations seeking to build, benchmark or augment their digital risk capability
